    What are AI notetakers?

    AI Notetakers automatically transcribe and summarize meetings, interviews, or conversations in real-time using artificial intelligence. These tools capture key points, action items, and decisions, allowing users to focus on the discussion instead of manual note-taking. AI Notetakers often integrate with video conferencing platforms and provide searchable transcripts, highlights, and even sentiment analysis.
